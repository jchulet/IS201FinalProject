About Me

My name is Jason Hulet and I am from Houston, Texas. I am finishing up my degree at BYU while working full-time doing FP&A for a tech company here in Utah County called Awardco, where I have been working for nearly two years. I love Texas, country music, playing and watching sports, skiing, fishing, hunting, and traveling.

I've seen Tim McGraw in concert three times, most recently at Stadium of Fire here in Provo last month.
I grew up fishing for bass and catfish in Texas, but this summer I've picked up fly fishing as a new pastime.
